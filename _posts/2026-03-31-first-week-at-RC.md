# What is the Recurse Center (aka RC)?
See this one-pager: <https://www.recurse.com>

# Why now, Leonid?
After being in a corporate environment for over 15 years, I got used to working in a community of
engineers.

The biggest impact of leaving that environment is the sudden loss of that community.

The Recurse Center brings that community back, but on much more generous terms.

# How am I planning to do it?
Reviving this blog is part of that! I plan to use a format I learned from my previous workplace:
<https://emauton.org/2015/11/05/snippets/> (Thanks Cian!).

The program lasts for 12 weeks, with a 6-week midpoint check-in/pivot.

# Risks
Pair programming is key to success. To make it work, I need to set aside chunks of time.
This is non-trivial, since I have family commitments.
